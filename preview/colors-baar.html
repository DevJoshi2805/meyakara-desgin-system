Contrast note — coral soul: baar-coral (#D98A78) on --paper (#F1ECE0) has a contrast ratio of ≈ 2.8:1. This fails WCAG AA for body-size text. Use coral for display type, decorative borders, and marks only. Reach for baar-tide (--accent-deep, #4F5E5C) when small text or interactive elements need colour on Baar pages.

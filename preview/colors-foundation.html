Foundation
Constant across every collection · never changes

The foundation is the brand at rest. Paper is the unbleached canvas. Ink is the mark that gives it shape. These vars are used on every surface in every collection. They never shift. The logo lives here and nowhere else. The lobby — header, footer, checkout — uses only these.

Paper — canvas
Paper
--paper
#F1ECE0
Primary canvas. All pages default to this.
Paper Warm
--paper-warm
#EDE6D4
Warmer paper for cards and raised sections.
Paper Cool
--paper-cool
#ECE9E1
Cooler variant. Subtle alternative surface.
Paper Deep
--paper-deep
#DDD5C2
Deepest paper. Annotation strips, sunken sections.
Ink — marks
Ink
--ink
#1A1814
Primary text, marks, hairlines. Never pure black.
Ink Soft
--ink-soft
#2B2722
Body copy when --ink reads too heavy.
Ink Mute
--ink-mute
#6B6359
Secondary text, captions, eyebrow labels.
Ink Faint
--ink-faint
#9C9384
Tertiary text, dividers within text, placeholders.
Ink Line
--ink-line
#1A1814
Hairline rule colour. Always 1px. Equals --ink.
Semantic aliases — use these in components, not the raw vars above
--bg
→ var(--paper)
Page background. Raat overrides to midnight.
--bg-raised
→ var(--paper-warm)
Raised surfaces — cards, tooltips.
--bg-sunken
→ var(--paper-deep)
Sunken or recessed sections.
--fg
→ var(--ink)
Primary foreground. Raat overrides to paper.
--fg-soft
→ var(--ink-soft)
Body text foreground.
--fg-mute
→ var(--ink-mute)
Captions, meta, eyebrow labels.
--fg-faint
→ var(--ink-faint)
Tertiary text, placeholders.
--rule
→ var(--ink)
Hairline rule colour. Use at 1px only.

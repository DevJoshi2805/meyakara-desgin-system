Nightlife · The Hour Before
Midnight black, electric violet, warm gold, the bleed. Colours of the hour before.
The escape that lives in the night — and comes back knowing exactly who she is.
Foundation inverts on this collection. Raat is the only collection that overrides --bg and --fg. This page is running dark to demonstrate that. On a real storefront, apply .collection-raat on the <main> element only — never on <header> or <footer>. The lobby (brand chrome) must always remain paper and ink.
